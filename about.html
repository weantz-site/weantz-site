About WeAntz

WeAntz provides plain-language know-your-rights guidance and connections to trusted local immigration, refugee, and community support organizations. Our focus is on peaceful, nonviolent community safety and constitutional rights.

Mission

Peace · Rights · Community · Constitution — supporting neighbors with resources and referrals so people can access legal help and safety when they need it most.

Disclaimer

This site shares general community resources and does not provide legal advice. For legal assistance, please contact a licensed attorney or the organizations listed on our pages.
